Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Who We Are

The SW6 Club is an unofficial fan-run prediction app. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

When you use the app, we may collect:

  • Account details: email address and authentication data.
  • Profile data: username, optional actual name, country, optional profile image.
  • Game data: leagues joined, predictions submitted, and related timestamps.
  • Basic technical data: browser/device info and logs needed for security and reliability.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To run leagues, predictions, leaderboards, and related features.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To improve app performance and product quality.

Service Providers

We use third-party providers to operate the service, including Supabase (authentication/database/storage) and Vercel (hosting/deployment). These providers process data on our behalf to deliver the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers needed to run the app, or when required by law.

Data Retention

We keep account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active, or as needed for legitimate operational and legal reasons.

Your Choices

  • You can update your profile information from the Edit Profile page.
  • You can request account/data deletion by contacting us at the email below.

Children

The service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: privacy@thesw6club.com
